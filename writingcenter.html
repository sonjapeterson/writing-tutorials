The Yale College Writing Center

The Yale College Writing Center is located at 35 Broadway (off of York St. near Toad's Place and the path to Morse and Stiles Colleges.) Undergraduate and graduate Writing Partners provide a walk-in service available 3-5 p.m. and 7-9 p.m. Sunday through Thursday, and 3-5 p.m. on Fridays. Writing Partners can help you at any stage of the writing process, from brainstorming a thesis to polishing a final draft. Show up any time during open hours with a printed copy of your essay to receive help.

Yale also provides writing assistance through the Residential College Writing Tutors. You must book appointments with these tutors and send them your writing in advance. Find out more or schedule an appointment at their website.

Visit the Yale College Writing Center website for more information about their services, as well as a variety of writing resources.
