Why: Anyone can plunk a quote in the middle of a paragraph. But in order to write a strong paper, you need to know how to have a conversation with the person you're quoting, and engage with it as part of building your own argument.

What: This tutorial will walk you through introducing, analyzing, and building off of a quote from a secondary source in four steps.

When: This tutorial will be helpful as you write and revise your body paragraphs, integrating secondary sources into your argument.
