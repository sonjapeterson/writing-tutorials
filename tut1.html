Testing your thesis

Why: Making sure you have a clear, strong thesis in the early stages of your writing process can save you a lot of time spent banging your head on your desk later.

What: This tutorial will give you 4 simple tests to tell if your thesis is a workable one, and provide tips for refining it.

When: This tutorial can actually be helpful at any stage of the writing process. Some people find they need to do some writing before they arrive at a thesis, while others like to start out with a very clear idea of their entire argument.
