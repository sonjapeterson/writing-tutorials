Step Three

Is your thesis specific?

Your thesis shouldn't be a blanket statement you can use to describe any old text or topic. It should be a specific statement that shows you've thought about something unique.

To Do:

Try thinking of another text or topic similar to yours and rewrite your thesis, replacing the name of text you are addressing in your thesis with that text. If the thesis still makes some sense, then you should try to make your thesis more specific.

Example:

Not very specific: "Game of Thrones uses violence to shock viewers" could be rewritten as "Breaking Bad uses violence in unexpected ways to shock viewers."

More specific: :"The TV show Game of Thrones makes its violent scenes shocking with cinematic techniques and plot twists that subvert fantasy tropes and audience expectations, and in doing so critiques the way the genre has desensitized its audience to violence."

Try putting Breaking Bad in there and it doesn't work. That's a good sign, but this thesis could be made even more specific, however, as I'll show in the next step.
